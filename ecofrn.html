BEYOND CLEAN:

The earth is facing an environmental crisis. We must all do better to be more sustainable – and the beauty industry is no exception. That's why we're thrilled to introduce the next wave of Clean at Sephora: Clean + Planet Positive. Meet the most ambitious clean brands on a mission to change the beauty landscape, and the earth, for the better.

HERE'S WHAT IT MEANS WHEN YOU SEE OUR CLEAN + PLANET POSITIVE SEAL:
Clean Ingredients

Clean at Sephora approved. Formulated without phthalates, formaldehyde or formaldehyde releasers, oxybenzone and octinoxate, hydroquinone, triclosan, coal tar, methylisothiazolinone, insoluble plastic, microbeads.

See full list of excluded ingredients ▸

Climate Commitment

Brands must meet at least one of the following climate goals: carbon-neutral operations, greenhouse gas emissions reduction, or corporate office and owned or leased operations powered by 100% renewable energy.

Sustainable Sourcing

Cruelty-free, using only sustainability harvested palm oil and mica, no microplastics, and up to Sephora's CBD standards.


Responsible Packaging

Must reduce, recycle, and reimagine the way they package. That means: no single-use items or unnecessary materials. Designing with recyclability in mind. Innovating with refillable components, post-consumer recycled content (PCR), biomaterials, plant-based ink and more.


Environmental Giving

Donate at least 1% of profit to a high-impact, long-term giving program that is multiyear and ongoing, or be a Certified B-corporation.
